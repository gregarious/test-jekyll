Inherits from NSObject
Conforms to SAYAudioFocusGuard
Declared in SAYAudioTrackCoordinator.h

Overview

The SAYAudioTrackCoordinator is the nerve-center for all audio operations. It manages multiple SAYAudioTracks, giving each permission to present their events according to their priority.
